ABOUT CDI GIS CONSULTING

Our goal is to make GIS services attainable, affordable, and approachable.

Created to help your company achieve their goals.

We believe that GIS should be accessible to everyone, regardless of the size of the company. That's why we offer affordable pricing for various GIS services.

A little about who we are and how it all got started

CDI GSI Consulting was created by just three people; three students who joined forces for a project in a class called "Web Design and Programming". They felt that GIS and drones were pretty cool so why not make a company for it? Plus, they all had a nifty Basics Operations license to fly drones. The three of them felt that they should at least put it to good use.
